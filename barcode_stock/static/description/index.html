Scan and add products in a new Purchase in Odoo!!

When it comes to entering a large number of products, it can directly affect the company's productivity as it is a time taking task. Also, it increases the chances of human errors too as the details are entered manually. So, adding products by scanning the barcode makes it easy and a perfect way to complete work on time.

Odoo Inventory Barcode Scanning Module helps the user effortlessly use barcodes to deliver products in incoming & outgoing deliveries. It allows the Searching of existing products by barcode & internal reference of the product. All the products are added to the Inventory order with the help of a barcode.
